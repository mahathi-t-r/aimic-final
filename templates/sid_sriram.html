SID SRIRAM

Sid Sriram is a versatile Indian-American singer-songwriter known for his soulful voice and captivating performances that blend elements of Indian classical music with contemporary styles.

Kaadhal En Kaviye

Kaadal Yen Kaviye" from the Tamil movie "Kaadhal" is a soul-stirring composition that delves into the depths of love, with Sid Sriram's emotive vocals adding layers of passion and emotion to the song.

Inkem Inkem Inkem Kaavaale

Inkem Inkem Inkem Kaavaale" from the Telugu movie "Geetha Govindam" is a melodious track that resonates with listeners through its catchy tune and heartfelt lyrics, sung with grace by Sid Sriram.

Adiye

Adiye" from the Tamil movie "Kadal" is a soulful track that captivates listeners with its haunting melody and evocative lyrics. Sung by Sid Sriram, the song beautifully expresses the yearning and emotions of the protagonist, making it a memorable addition to the soundtrack.

Aaradya

"Aradhya" is a Telugu song from the movie Kushi. It's a powerful and emotive track that reflects the intensity of emotions. Sid Sriram's soulful rendition adds depth and passion to the song, making it a standout piece in the film's soundtrack.
