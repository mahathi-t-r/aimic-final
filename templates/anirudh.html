ANIRUDH RAVICHANDER

ANIRUDH RAVICHANDER IS A MUSICAL CHAMELEON OF SOUTH INDIAN CINEMA. HE BLENDS CATCHY TAMIL MELODIES WITH MODERN BEATS, CREATING A SOUND THAT'S BOTH INNOVATIVE AND IRRESISTIBLE. A TRUE TALENT WHO KEEPS AUDIENCES HOOKED

Baitikochi Chuste

"Baitikochi Chuste" from the movie "Agnyaathavaasi" is a peppy number composed by Anirudh Ravichander. The song features energetic vocals and catchy beats, making it a favorite among fans.

Vikram

The Vikram title track is a high-energy song sung by Anirudh Ravichander. It blends the vibe of the film's characters with a catchy beat, hinting at the action and mystery to come.

kanave kanave

A soulful melody by Anirudh Ravichander himself, "Kanave Kanave" explores themes of longing and searching. With melancholic vocals and emotional lyrics, it captures the protagonist's inner turmoil and yearning.

pove pove yekantham

A playful and romantic song from "Raghuvaran B Tech," "Po Pove Yekantham" is sung by Dhanunjay. Its whimsical lyrics and catchy tune express the protagonist's feelings of infatuation and newfound love.
