Devi Sri Prasad

Devi Sri Prasad, commonly known as DSP, is a renowned music composer in the Indian film industry. He is celebrated for his energetic compositions and catchy tunes that have become instant hits.

Aa Ante Amalapuram

"Aa Ante Amalapuram" is a pulsating dance number composed by Devi Sri Prasad. It gained immense popularity for its catchy beats and energetic choreography.

Ringa Ringa

"Ringa Ringa" from the movie "Arya 2" is a peppy track composed by Devi Sri Prasad. Its catchy rhythm and lively vocals make it a favorite among music lovers.

Follow Follow

"Follow Follow" is a lively and upbeat song from the movie "Nannaku Prematho," composed by Devi Sri Prasad. It features energetic vocals and catchy beats that make it a favorite among listeners

Yenta Sakkagunnave

Yenta Sakkagunnave from the Telugu movie Rangastalam is a melodious track that resonates with listeners through its catchy tune and beautiful lyrics by Devi sri prasad
